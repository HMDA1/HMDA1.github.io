三、STRUCTURE

重中之重。这里的结构不是指该写四段还是五段，而是一种PEE的写作逻辑和如何保证cohesion & coherence（官方评分标准中强调的连贯度）

1、 PEE
Point-explain-example
这是dominic的详细讲解Paragraph coherence in IELTS writing
简单说来就是给出观点（一句话），解释观点（一两句话），给出一个例子。

但这并不是固定模式。比如我在分析dominic的范文时，发现有些没有例子，那么解释的部分就长一些。有些观点有两层，那么大观点（point）可以单独列出，比如说“某某东西是值得肯定的”，小观点就可以和explain直接混为一体，不用单独再列一个小point出来，这样的段落通常是（大point–>小point1+explain1–>example1–>小point2+explain2–>example2）的模式，往往出现在两个小Point是可以用大point来概括的时候；而如果两个小point相对独立，无法用一句话加以概括，就可以不用大point来打头阵，这样的段落就可以是（point1–>explain1–>example1–>point2–>explain2–>example2）
这不是死板的模式，可以根据具体情况灵活变通，关键就在于先明晰观点再加以阐述，最好来个例子。

Ideas for IELTS topics

Who is this book for?

Many students have no ideas or opinions about IETLS writing topics. Even if your

grammar is perfect, you will not get a high IELTS score if you do not know what to write.

This book is for students who want to go into the writing exam feeling confident that they have excellent ideas, opinions and vocabulary for as many topics as possible

How do you get an IELTS score of 7 or higher?

The key to a high score is not grammar, it is not your use of words like “moreover” or “in addition”, and it is not your use of phrases like “this is controversial issue nowadays”.

To get a high score you need to use good “topic language” (words and phrases

that are related to the question topic). You need good ideas in order to answer the

question well.

How should you prepare for IELTS writing task 2?

There is an enormous amount of advice on my website ielts-simon.com. Here is a

summary of what I suggest:

  • Spend more time preparing than testing

When you test yourself, you find out what your level is, but you do not learn anything

new. You will not improve if you only write test essays. Before writing an essay,

study the topic and prepare your ideas, opinions and vocabulary.

  • Steal my ideas

If you only use the vocabulary that you already know, you will not learn anything

new, and you will not improve. Practise linking my topic ideas together to write

“perfect essays”. See the website for examples of how to do this.

  • Use this book together with the website

The website ielts-simon.com contains lessons and videos that show you how to use

the ideas from this book. You can speak to me there if you have any questions.
